Helles Game Documentation

Introduction

This document describes a game called “Helles”, which is designed to be a simple, 2D side-scroller game. The player of this game is using a bow as the default weapon, defeating monsters and finding keys to open doors. The player will be able to upgrade his weapon and get new abilities as the game progresses.

Technology

Helles is a 2D side-scroller game that builds upon McKilla’s Gorilla Wolfies 2D game engine. Helles will be employed on a global entry website using Firebase. Webgl will be used for rendering effects and updating the view of each frame.

Back Story

In the heart of the dense and ominous Helles Woods lies a tale of survival and courage. You are thrust into the role of a skilled hunter, a solitary figure venturing into the unknown depths of the forest. What begins as a routine expedition quickly turns into a harrowing ordeal as you find yourself ensnared in the treacherous labyrinth of Helles.

Armed only with your trusty bow and a scant few provisions, you embark on a desperate quest for survival. Every corner of Helles is fraught with danger, from the vicious denizens that lurk in the shadows to the twisting paths that lead to unknown fates. Yet, with each encounter, you grow stronger, honing your skills and resourcefulness in the face of overwhelming odds.

But amidst the chaos and despair, there is a glimmer of hope. With each monster felled and each obstacle overcome, you inch ever closer to unlocking the secrets of Helles Woods. And though the path ahead may be treacherous, you are determined to emerge victorious, to defy the odds and carve your own destiny in the annals of legend.

For in the realm of Helles, where darkness reigns supreme, only the boldest and bravest souls dare to tread. And it is up to you, the intrepid hunter, to face the challenges that lie ahead and emerge triumphant against all odds.

Objective

At the beginning, all the doors will be closed and all the keys will be guarded by some powerful monster. You have to defeat those monsters to open the doors. Only one door will be your way out, and for the other doors, you will find items to upgrade your weapon or supplies that will help you defeat monsters. The monster that guards the key for the way out will be very powerful. You want to first explore other doors to get supplies and upgrade weapons before you try to kill the final monster.

          In “Helles”, players embark on a thrilling journey through the treacherous depths of the enchanted forest, where danger lurks at every turn. Below is the breakdown for the game mechanics. 
          1.Exploration and Map:
          
            Player explores the side-scrolling environment of Helles Woods. The forest is filled with branching paths, hidden secrets and deadly traps, encouraging exploration and discovery.  
          
          2.Monster Encounters: 
          
            Throughout the game, player will encounter various monsters. Some monsters guard keys that unlock doors blocking the player’s path.
          
          3.Key and Door unlocking: 
          
            Initially, all doors barring the player’s progress are sealed shut, you have to defeat a monster holding a corresponding key in order to open the door. Only one door leads to the ultimate goal of escape, while the others contain valuable upgrades and supplies to aid the player in the journey.
